Step By Step Journey to Restore your Hair

The Magic behind your Transformation

Diagnosis

Start with a 45-60 minute consultation to diagnose your hair loss condition. We will work with you to understand your hair goals and evaluate whether a hair transplant is right for you. There are plenty of non-surgical hair loss treatments as well to choose from.

Procedure Day

This is your big day. Your DFI surgery will take place in the comfort of our clinic and your surgical experience is guaranteed to be safe, painless and relaxing. It will take 6-8 hours and is an outpatient procedure. In larger cases, we carry forward the procedure to the second day.

After Care

Now, wait and watch your transformation unfold. Work can be resumed in 3 days. You’ll see new hair growth start in 6 months and full head of hair in 12-18 months.
